Renewable, clean burning masonry heaters for a healthier home

Masonry Heaters do not require EPA certification due to their inherent clean burning and efficient heating. Even though the technology has been in existence for hundreds of years, they are the future technology of wood burning heaters.

Tile Stoves

Call it a Kachelofen, Piec Kaflowy, Kakkelovn, Kakelugn, or Tiled Stove. It goes by many names but whatever you call it, we are focused on providing the best performing, unique and beautiful wood burning heaters.

How does it work?

Masonry heater construction allows the fuel to burn hotter and more completely. Very little pollution and very little ash is left over. The masonry surrounding the firebox and flue channels inside the stove absorb most of the heat from the fire and slowly release a pleasant radiant heat into the room for 12-24 hours. Ceramic tiles constructed specially for each heater is assembled around the core of the masonry heater. The tiles act as additional heat storage mass that allows the heater to gently radiate the warmth to the room safely and cleanly.
