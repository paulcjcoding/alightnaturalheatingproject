Tile Stoves

Centuries old design into the modern home



How does it work?

Masonry heater construction allows the fuel to burn hotter and more completely. Very little pollution and very little ash is left over. The masonry surrounding the firebox and flue channels inside the stove absorb most of the heat from the fire and slowly release a pleasant radiant heat into the room for 12-24 hours

The Tiles

Ceramic tiles constructed specially for each heater is assembled around the core of the masonry heater. The tiles act as additional heat storage mass that allows the heater to gently radiate the warmth to the room safely and cleanly.
